## ROI Calculator Guide

### How to Calculate Your ROI

**Step 1: Calculate Lost Revenue from Missed Calls**

```
Formula:
Missed Calls/Month √ó Conversion Rate √ó Average Sale Value = Lost Revenue

Example:
40 missed calls √ó 40% conversion √ó $150 = $2,400/month
```

**Variables:**
- **Missed Calls:** Estimate based on calls to voicemail, busy signals, or peak time estimates
- **Conversion Rate:** Industry average 30-50%; be conservative with 40%
- **Average Sale Value:** Your typical transaction value

---

**Step 2: Calculate Staff Time Savings**

```
Formula:
(Hours saved per week) √ó (Hourly staff cost) √ó 4.33 weeks = Monthly Value

Example:
10 hours √ó $24/hour √ó 4.33 = $1,039/month
```

**How to Estimate Hours Saved:**
- Count routine calls that AI would handle
- Average 2-3 minutes per routine call
- Multiply by number of routine calls per week

---

**Step 3: Calculate Total Monthly Benefit**

```
Formula:
Lost Revenue Captured + Staff Time Savings = Total Monthly Benefit

Example:
$2,400 + $1,039 = $3,439/month
```

---

**Step 4: Calculate ROI**

```
Formula:
(Total Monthly Benefit - AI System Cost) √∑ AI System Cost = ROI

Example:
($3,439 - $497) √∑ $497 = 5.9x ROI
```

---

**Step 5: Calculate Payback Period**

```
Formula:
AI System Cost √∑ (Total Monthly Benefit √∑ 30 days) = Days to Payback

Example:
$497 √∑ ($3,439 √∑ 30) = 4.3 days
```
