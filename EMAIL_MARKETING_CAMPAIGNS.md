## A/B Testing Ideas

**Subject Lines:**
- Question vs. statement
- Personalization vs. generic
- Urgency vs. value-focused
- Emoji vs. no emoji
- Length (short vs. descriptive)

**CTAs:**
- Button text variations
- Single CTA vs. multiple options
- Placement (top vs. bottom vs. both)

**Content:**
- Short vs. long format
- Story-based vs. facts-based
- Pain-first vs. solution-first

**Send Times:**
- Morning (8-10am) vs. afternoon (2-4pm)
- Weekday vs. weekend
- Industry-specific optimal times
