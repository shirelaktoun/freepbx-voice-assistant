## Social Media Strategy Overview

### Platform Priority & Focus

**LinkedIn (Primary B2B):**
- **Audience:** Business owners, office managers, IT directors
- **Content:** Thought leadership, case studies, ROI data, industry insights
- **Frequency:** 3 posts/week + daily engagement
- **Goal:** Lead generation, brand credibility

**Facebook (Primary SMB):**
- **Audience:** Small business owners, especially automotive, home services
- **Content:** Customer success stories, tips, community building
- **Frequency:** 5 posts/week + ad campaigns
- **Goal:** Community building, local business reach

**Instagram:**
- **Audience:** Younger business owners, lifestyle-focused
- **Content:** Behind-the-scenes, customer testimonials, visual storytelling
- **Frequency:** 3-5 posts/week + daily stories
- **Goal:** Brand awareness, humanization

**Twitter/X:**
- **Audience:** Tech-savvy users, industry news followers
- **Content:** Quick tips, industry news, engagement
- **Frequency:** 2-3 posts/day
- **Goal:** Real-time engagement, customer service

**YouTube:**
- **Audience:** Research-focused buyers, DIY implementers
- **Content:** Tutorials, demos, long-form content
- **Frequency:** 1 video/week
- **Goal:** Education, SEO, lead nurture
